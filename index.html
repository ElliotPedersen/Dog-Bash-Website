Dog Bash

About Dog Bash

Dog Bash is a application for your phone that helps with your dogtraining. It is meant to be used as a tool for when you want to practice meeting other dogs or dog owners with your dog. You can share your location with other owners in your area by clicking on the "Share Location" button and then send push notifications to them and ask if they want to practice with you. It can also be used as a tool if you currently don't want to be approached by another dog by simply stating that in your profile. For example if your dog is reactive you can put that in your profile so other owners know what to do and what to expect. Sometimes you might want to practice just passing another dog, just send a notification to another owner and ask if they are interested in practicing with you. Maybe that is the beginning of you and your dog finding your new best friends.
